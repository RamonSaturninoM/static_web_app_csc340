Red Panda

Species: *Ailurus fulgens*

Habitat: Temperate forests of the Himalayas and China

The red panda is a small arboreal mammal known for its russet fur and ringed tail. Despite its name, it is not closely related to the giant panda. Red pandas are crepuscular, spending much of the day resting in trees and feeding mainly on bamboo, fruits, and small insects.

  • Average lifespan: 8–10 years (wild)
  • Diet: Mostly bamboo; also fruits and insects
  • Conservation status: Endangered
